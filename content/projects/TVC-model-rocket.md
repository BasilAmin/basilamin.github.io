## Why I built it

I wanted to build a high-performance model rocket using the TVC mechanism for precise flight control and stability.

## How it works

The rocket uses a TVC system where the thrust vector is controlled by rotating the engine mount or gimbal assembly during flight. This allows for real-time adjustments to the flight trajectory without relying on aerodynamic surfaces like fins or canards. The design focuses on lightweight construction, efficient aerodynamics, and precise control mechanisms to achieve stable, high-altitude flights.
