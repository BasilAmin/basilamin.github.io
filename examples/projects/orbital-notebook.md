## Brief

Orbital mechanics is full of ideas that are easier to understand when they move. This project is a collection of small, focused visual tools for studying paths, periods, and transfer geometry without burying the useful part under a large interface.

## Design approach

- Keep every view centred on one question.
- Show units and assumptions beside the result.
- Make diagrams useful before adding animation.
- Treat the browser as a notebook, not a dashboard.

## What I learned

The best improvement was not another control. It was making the default state explain itself. A useful technical tool should let someone form an accurate first impression before they touch anything.
