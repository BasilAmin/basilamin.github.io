A small tool has an advantage that is easy to underestimate: you can finish it before the problem changes.

Large projects invite architecture, accounts, dashboards, and imagined future users. A focused tool can begin with a single sentence: **take this awkward task and remove three steps**.

## A useful test

Before adding a feature, ask whether it strengthens the original sentence. When the answer is no, write the idea down somewhere else.

The result may look less impressive in a screenshot, but it is far more likely to become part of your actual life. That is a better measure of success.
