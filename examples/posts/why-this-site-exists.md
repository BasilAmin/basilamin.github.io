Most places online are built around a feed. That is useful for discovery, but it is a bad shape for keeping a body of work.

This site is meant to be slower and more permanent. Projects can have proper context. Small observations can live in the log. Longer ideas can become essays when they deserve the space.

## The rule

The site should make publishing easier than postponing. That means plain files, obvious folders, and no system that needs regular maintenance just to keep old writing online.

## What belongs here

- Work I can explain, including unfinished work.
- Notes that may be useful again.
- Questions I am still thinking about.
- Links and references worth preserving.

No feed, no engagement counter, and no pressure to turn every thought into content. Just a place that accumulates evidence of what I care about.
